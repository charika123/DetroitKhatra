ABOUT US

Detroit Khatra is Michigan's newest bollywood-fusion dance team formed from the former talents of WSU Jhatka and Detroit Kohinoor that's based out of Detroit, MI. Composed of students from the Wayne State and metro Detroit area, Detroit Khatra strives to push the creative limits of various dance styles (including bollywood, bhangra, hip hop, classical, contemporary, and more) to create a beautiful fusion of dance that illustrates not only a story, but a message as well.
